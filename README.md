**Connecting Students, Colleges, and Companies**

## Overview
Rojgar Setu is a career and employment platform designed to bridge the gap between students, colleges, and companies. The platform focuses on improving student employability through AI-powered resume and portfolio creation while simplifying recruitment and placement processes.

---

## Objectives
- Enable students to create AI-generated resumes and portfolios
- Connect colleges and companies on a single platform
- Improve career readiness of students
- Streamline hiring and placement workflows

---

## User Roles
- Student
- College
- Company

Each role has a dedicated dashboard with role-based access.

---

## Student Section
### Features
- **AI Resume Maker**
  - ATS-friendly resume generation
  - Skill-based suggestions
  - Multiple templates

- **AI Portfolio Maker**
  - Automatic portfolio creation
  - Project, skill, and certification showcase
  - AI-generated content

- **Student Profile**
  - Academic information
  - Skills and interests
  - Projects and internships

- **Job & Internship Applications**
  - Apply to opportunities
  - Track application status

---

## College Section
- Manage and verify student profiles
- Monitor placements and career progress
- Coordinate with companies
- Access placement analytics

---

## Company Section
- Post jobs and internships
- View student profiles
- Shortlist and contact students

---

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend:**  React , typescript
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express
- **Database:** MongoDB / MySQL
- **AI:** Machine Learning, NLP
- **Authentication:** Role-based secure JWT login

## Security
- Secure authentication
